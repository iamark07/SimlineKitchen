Our Story

Est. 2002

At Simline Kitchen, we've been transforming spaces and creating dream kitchens for over a decade. Our journey began with a simple vision: to bring luxury and functionality together in every kitchen we design. Known widely for manufacturing, wholesaling and supplying a quality assured array of Kitchen and Wardrobe Accessories, we "Slimline (a Product Of Kitchens)" started our operation in the year of 2002. In short span of time we have gained a distinct position in the market by offering Baskets, Steel Bins and SS Pullouts. These accessories are manufactured and designed by using top quality materials by our experienced professionals with the help of our top notch tools and technology. We are offering these accessories in various specifications to suit the diverse needs and demands of our clients in most effective manner. Our offered accessories are highly appreciated in the market for their excellent finishing, designs and longer service life.
