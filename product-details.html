Product Overview

The Bottle Basket is a premium kitchen solution designed to maximize storage efficiency and accessibility for your bottles. Constructed from high-quality annealed stainless steel with a durable Nickel Chrome finish, it offers exceptional longevity and resistance to rust and corrosion. Compatible with modern telescopic slides, this basket ensures smooth and effortless pull-out action, allowing easy access to items stored deep within your cabinets. With a robust loading capacity of 25 Kgs, it can safely store multiple bottles of various sizes. Its versatile design makes it suitable for cabinet widths ranging from 450mm to 900mm, providing flexible storage options for different kitchen layouts. Enhance your kitchen organization with this stylish and functional bottle basket.
